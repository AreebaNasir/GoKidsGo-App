# GoKidsGo-App  
# Introduction  
GoKidsGo is an android application inspired by the concept of introducing kids to e-learning at an early age as part of their learning experience. It targets schools that want to innovate their teaching process and change the traditional means of teaching as promoting and implementing e-learning at the kindergarten level is crucial to shaping childrenâ€™s educational prospects positively.  

# Project Scope  
The primary goal of GoKidsGo is to provide young students (3-5 age group) with a wonderful platform for learning where they can seek new ideas and insights about topics they learn through communication and collaboration.
